Quick Formulas

The official Grade 12 NSC Mathematics Information Sheet. You will be provided with this sheet in your final exams.

Algebra, Sequences & Series

Quadratic Formula
\( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)
Constant Sequence Sum
\( \sum_{i=1}^{n} 1 = n \)
Sum of First n Integers
\( \sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2} \)
Arithmetic Series Sum
\( \sum_{i=1}^{n} (a + (i-1)d) = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d] \)
Geometric Series Sum
\( \sum_{i=1}^{n} ar^{i-1} = \frac{a(r^n - 1)}{r-1} \ ; \ r \neq 1 \)
Sum to Infinity
\( \sum_{i=1}^{\infty} ar^{i-1} = \frac{a}{1-r} \ ; \ -1 < r < 1 \)

Finance, Growth & Decay

Simple Interest / Growth
\( A = P(1 + ni) \)
Simple Depreciation
\( A = P(1 - ni) \)
Compound Depreciation
\( A = P(1 - i)^n \)
Compound Interest
\( A = P(1 + i)^n \)
Future Value Annuity
\( F = \frac{x[(1+i)^n - 1]}{i} \)
Present Value Annuity
\( P = \frac{x[1-(1+i)^{-n}]}{i} \)

Differential Calculus

First Principles
\( f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h} \)

Analytical Geometry

Distance Formula
\( d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \)
Midpoint
\( M \left(\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2} ; \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}\right) \)
Straight Line (Gradient-Intercept)
\( y = mx + c \)
Straight Line (Point-Gradient)
\( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \)
Gradient (Two Points)
\( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)
Angle of Inclination
\( m = \tan \theta \)
Circle Equation
\( (x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 = r^2 \)

Trigonometry

Sine Rule
\( \frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} \)
Cosine Rule
\( a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc \cos A \)
Area Rule
\( \text{area } \Delta ABC = \frac{1}{2}ab \sin C \)
Compound Angle - Sine (+)
\( \sin(\alpha + \beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta + \cos \alpha \sin \beta \)
Compound Angle - Sine (-)
\( \sin(\alpha - \beta) = \sin \alpha \cos \beta - \cos \alpha \sin \beta \)
Compound Angle - Cosine (+)
\( \cos(\alpha + \beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta - \sin \alpha \sin \beta \)
Compound Angle - Cosine (-)
\( \cos(\alpha - \beta) = \cos \alpha \cos \beta + \sin \alpha \sin \beta \)
Double Angle - Sine
\( \sin 2\alpha = 2\sin \alpha \cos \alpha \)
Double Angle - Cosine
\( \cos 2\alpha = \begin{cases} \cos^2 \alpha - \sin^2 \alpha \\ 1 - 2\sin^2 \alpha \\ 2\cos^2 \alpha - 1 \end{cases} \)

Statistics & Probability

Mean (\( \bar{x} \))
\( \bar{x} = \frac{\sum fx}{n} \)
Variance (\( \sigma^2 \))
\( \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum_{i=1}^{n}(x_i - \bar{x})^2}{n} \)
Basic Probability
\( P(A) = \frac{n(A)}{n(S)} \)
Addition Rule
\( P(A \text{ or } B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A \text{ and } B) \)
Least Squares Regression Line
\( \hat{y} = a + bx \)
Regression Slope (b)
\( b = \frac{\sum(x - \bar{x})(y - \bar{y})}{\sum(x - \bar{x})^2} \)
